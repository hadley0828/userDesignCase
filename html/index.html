Apron

Overview

Nowadays, the world is experiencing an unprecedented pandemic. People used to go outside for eating, but now the ways to get food are much different. However, it is difficult for many people to cook food at the same caliber as restaurant cuisine because of their lack of experience. Our team aims to design a system that can guide people to be a better cook, and can provide support for staying-at-home people a way to ease into cooking.

“We want to design a system that helps people ease into cooking”
